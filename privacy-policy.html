Privacy Policy

Whats Web Dual Messenger App

With the given policy, we notify the laws, rules, limitations, conditions, and terms of services when you use our app.

1. Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the "Whats Web Dual Messenger App" mobile application (the "App"), published by Dixit Kanpariya. We respect your privacy and this Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

2. What the App Does

Whats Web Dual Messenger App lets you run multiple parallel accounts (such as WhatsApp Web and other supported services) inside isolated in-app browser sessions on a single device, along with built-in utility tools (QR code creator, message scheduler, secret notes, emoji and text tools, etc.).

3. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Account/instance names and labels you create inside the App (stored only on your device).
  • Content you create using in-app tools (e.g. notes, scheduled messages, QR codes) — stored locally on your device.

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • App usage and interaction events, device/OS type, and crash diagnostics, collected via Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, to help us understand usage and fix bugs.
  • Subscription/purchase status, processed by our in-app purchase provider (RevenueCat) and the App Store, in order to unlock premium features. We do not collect or store your payment card details.

c. Third-Party Account Sessions

When you log into a third-party service (e.g. WhatsApp Web, Instagram, Telegram) inside the App's in-app browser, that service handles your login and chat data directly under its own privacy policy. We do not access, intercept, or store the content of those sessions.

4. Biometric / Passcode Lock

If you enable App Lock or the Secret Vault feature, your device's biometric authentication (Face ID / fingerprint) or a passcode you set is used to restrict access. Biometric data is processed entirely by your device's operating system and is never sent to us or stored on our servers.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App and its features.
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs.
  • To manage premium subscriptions and unlock purchased features.
  • To provide customer support when you contact us.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising. The App does not display ads.

6. How Information May Be Shared

  • Service providers: Firebase (Google) for analytics/crash reporting, and RevenueCat for subscription management. Each processes data under their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users.
  • We do not share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

7. Data Storage and Security

Most of your App data (account instances, notes, settings) is stored locally on your device. We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect any data that is processed, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Your Choices

  • You can disable analytics collection permission in your device settings where applicable.
  • You can delete an account instance or local data at any time from within the App.
  • You can uninstall the App at any time, which removes all locally stored data.
